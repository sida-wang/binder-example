Bore plot example

Instructions

The plot below allows for the filtering of the Victorian boreholes through various properties.
Each property can be filtered individually by first clicking the "Inactive" button to make them "Active" and then modifying the range as desired.

The toolbar on the right of the plot displays the available tools and allows the user to turn them on and off as desired.
Going down from the top we have:
Pan tool: Click and drag to move the map around.
Box zoom: Click and drag to form a box to zoom the map into.
Wheel zoom: Scroll on the map to zoom in or out. Scroll on either axis to manipulate the aspect ratio.
Tap tool: Click on a borehole to open up the borehole profile in another tab.
Reset: Reset the map to the original view.
Hover tool: Shows the variables as you hover over each borehole.
